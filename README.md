# Performance-Marketing-Data-Analysis
📊 Marketing Performance Analysis | Key Insights & Strategy Shift
Analyzed recent multi-channel marketing data to evaluate not just growth—but efficiency.
Here are the key takeaways:
Facebook generated the highest number of ads, leading in activity volume.
Email Marketing delivered the highest ROI at 15.59 — the most efficient channel.
Retargeting Campaigns achieved the highest conversion rates, showing strong intent capture.
Important Insight:
Higher ad spend ≠ higher returns
LinkedIn had the highest ad spend (₹8.71L)
But delivered the lowest ROI (1.28)
Meanwhile:
Email had the lowest spend (₹1.90L)
Yet achieved the highest ROI (15.59)
Strategic Recommendation:
Reallocate budget towards Email Marketing to maximize returns and scale revenue efficiently.
Tools & Techniques Used:
Data Modeling
DAX
Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts
Focus: Moving from spend-driven decisions to efficiency-driven marketing
